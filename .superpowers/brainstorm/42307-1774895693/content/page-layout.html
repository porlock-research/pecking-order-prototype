Key Design Decisions

  • Hero: Persona images (actual R2 images, not emoji) with game title + tagline
  • Teaser strip: 4 action words that hint at gameplay without revealing mechanics
  • Form: Just 2 fields — email + referral source dropdown (with "Other" free text)
  • CTA button: Gold with 3D press effect (matches ui-kit btn-press)
  • "Playtesting Now" pill: Creates urgency without a specific date
  • No nav bar: Single-purpose page, no distractions
  • Social meta: OG image, Twitter card, Discord embed (not shown — server-rendered)
