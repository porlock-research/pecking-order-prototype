Fullscreen Flow

  • Entry: Tap CTA on LIVE card → slides up to fullscreen (covers tab bar)
  • Header: X dismiss button, cartridge name, live countdown
  • Body: The actual cartridge component (VotingPanel, GamePanel, etc.) — same components as today
  • Dismiss: X button or swipe down → returns to Today tab. Player can come back anytime while still live.
  • Auto-close: When cartridge completes, fullscreen dismisses and card transitions to Completed state
  • Tab bar hidden during fullscreen — player is focused on the cartridge
