Which improvements matter most for tomorrow?

Multi-select — click all that apply

1

Fix action language per mechanism

Bubble: "Save a player" not "vote". Tally label: "2 saves" not just "2". Confirmation: "You saved Ember!" not "Vote cast!". Majority stays as-is (it's already clear). Low effort, high clarity impact.

2

Collapsible rules banner at top of panel

Slim bar showing mechanism summary from VOTE_TYPE_INFO.howItWorks. Expanded by default, collapsible. Always accessible — no more forgetting rules after splash.

3

Voter participation strip

Row of persona avatars at the top showing all eligible voters. Green ring = voted, dim = pending. Players can see participation in real-time. Creates social pressure to vote.

4

Full visual redesign via frontend-design skill

Complete redesign of voting panels with better hierarchy, mechanism-specific theming, polished animations, and modern mobile-first layout. Biggest effort, biggest impact.

5

Result persistence (systemic — benefits all cartridges)

When voting/game/activity/dilemma completes, the result snapshot stays visible in Today's Lineup (Schedule tab). Spotlight card expands in-place to show rich results. Uses existing completedPhases data. Server-side: no changes. Client-side: enhanced TimelineEventCard rendering.
