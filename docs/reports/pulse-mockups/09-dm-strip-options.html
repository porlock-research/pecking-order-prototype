At a glance

F — Two-Zone Strip

One strip with a thin vertical divider between people and rooms. Groups get text chips, not portrait stacks.

+ Everything on one row · single tap · no hidden surfaces
− Strip can feel dense at crowded load · mixes two visual languages

G — Groups Drawer

Strip stays pure people. "Groups•3" chip opens a drawer with rich rows (preview, members).

+ Strip stays lean · groups get room to breathe · scales to N groups
− Extra tap to reach a group · drawer introduces modal surface

H — Pinned Group Cards

Strip is 1:1 only. Groups live as cards at top of the chat scroll — scroll away when not needed.

+ Puts groups where convo happens · scales vertically not horizontally
− Eats chat real-estate · may look like a pinned-post banner
